"Rack Management System (RMS) - A rack shelf organizer with features to define your product, arrange them as they are at your branch, and set alerts as per your requirements."

Features

Rack Management is an online application designed to manage shelf racks available at your workplace. It helps to organize items and products and thus reduce the return time from the racks to serve the needy in a better way. Admin and staff play prominent roles in the applications. Admin can define categories, staff, products, and racks. And staff are allowed to retrieve items from the shelf racks. It has notification settings to receive notifications when items or products cross a defined limit.

Manage Stores

The admin user can manage the stores across various locations. Staff users are assigned to the stores; similarly, shelf racks belong to the stores.The store can contain a number of products, items, and racks where all the products are added to the corresponding racks. Notifications can also be created for the store to notify them of the low quantity of products.

Define Categories

The administrator shall be allowed to define specifications for their products and items. They will be able to drag and drop input form fields to define the various categories of products and items that they manage. These fields will be available while adding or editing products and items in the store.

Translate Phrases

It allows you to rephrase the words that are used in the application to match your business terminology. For instance, you can rename the phrase `Product` as `Appliances`, similarly, `Rack` as `Cub Board` and `Shelf` as `Tray` etc. Once a phrase is translated, it is available to its admin and staff across their various stores.

Rack View

A shelf rack view shows all the trays and cubboards that they contain. You can also customize your tray by adding a picture to it, changing the color, renaming the tray, copying it, or deleting it. You can also arrange your trays as you like by dragging them to expand or shrink. You can select a tray to know the products and items that it contains.

Check out

From the rack view, users will be able to access the shelves and tracks. When a shelf or track is opened, all the items and products that are on it will be listed by their categories. From this view, users can navigate to the check-in process to add items and products to the shelf or tray.

Notifications

The user can define alerts to notify them when products or items reach a lower quantity limit on a shelf or track. It can be configured with the list of emails to be notified, the number of reminders, and the time interval between the reminders.
